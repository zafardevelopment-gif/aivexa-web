Our Story

Veloura Living was founded on a simple belief: every home deserves furniture that combines beauty, comfort, and craftsmanship.

Founded on Passion

In 2010, Veloura Living was born from a passion for craftsmanship and a vision to bring premium, made-to-measure sofas to Indian homes. Our founders set out to create sofas that would become heirlooms.

What started as a small workshop with a handful of artisans has grown into a thriving studio — but our core values remain unchanged: quality, integrity, and a deep respect for the craft.

Today, Veloura Living serves homeowners, interior designers, architects and hospitality businesses across India, each a testament to our commitment to excellence.

Our Manufacturing Philosophy

We believe in slow, intentional craftsmanship that prioritises quality over quantity.
